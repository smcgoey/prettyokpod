Ep. 393: And Then There Were Seven

Happy Thanksgiving, y'all — Sean, Pierce, and Kevin got together before going home for the holidays to talk about the 7 remaining undefeated FBS teams and what would be the best (or funniest) outcomes for each.

Of course, that was before any of the games were played. So tune in to see how many of the things they said are already outdated!
