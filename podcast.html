Ep. 355: Our First MVP Pod Ever (Don't Fact Check That)

This week, the Sports Bois are together again to do something we've definitely never done in the history of this podcast. That's right, we're talking about MVP awards and what they mean. Okay, fine, we've done this before. Like, kind of a lot. But this time, the catalyst is different: the extremely likely possibility that Nuggets center Nikola Jokic is going to win his third consecutive NBA MVP award — a relatively unprecedented feat given that it's only happened three other times. Oh, and we get in an argument over whether a gas-powered leaf blower emits as many chemicals in half an hour as a pickup truck does driving from New York to Los Angeles.
